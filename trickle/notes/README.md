# Review & Testimonial Capture App

An automated review capture and management platform for service-based businesses.

## Core Features
1. **Automated Capture**: Easily send clients a link to leave a review.
2. **Review Routing**: 4-5 star reviews are encouraged to be posted publicly (e.g., Google, Yelp), while 1-3 star reviews are routed to private feedback.
3. **Dashboard Analytics**: Business owners can track their average rating, total reviews, and capture conversion rate.

## Pages
- `index.html`: Public landing page explaining the product.
- `dashboard.html`: The internal dashboard for business owners to manage reviews.
- `capture.html`: The public-facing form where clients leave their testimonials and ratings.
